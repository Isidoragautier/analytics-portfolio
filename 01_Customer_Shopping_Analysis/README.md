# Customer Shopping Behaviour Analysis

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SciPy  
**Dataset:** Istanbul shopping mall transactions (99,000+ rows)

## What this project does
End-to-end data analysis pipeline including data cleaning, outlier detection, 
trend analysis, and business recommendations.

## Key findings
- Clothing, Shoes and Technology are the top 3 spending categories
- No significant age-spending correlation across any category
- Cash is the most common payment method; credit card users spend slightly more per transaction
- Recommended marketing focus: Technology category + credit card campaigns

## Skills demonstrated
Data cleaning, date parsing, IQR outlier detection, EDA, correlation analysis, 
time series visualisation, business insight communication
